Required Settings

Plex

+

Enter the URL of your Plex server, including http:// or https:// and the port number if necessary.

Enter your Plex authentication token. This can be found by following the instructions at https://support.plex.tv/articles/204059436-finding-an-authentication-token-x-plex-token/

Enter the names of your Plex movie libraries, separated by commas.

Enter the names of your Plex TV show libraries, separated by commas.

Overseerr

+

Enter the URL of your Overseerr instance, including http:// or https:// and the port number if necessary.

Enter your Overseerr API key. This can be found in your Overseerr settings.

Real Debrid

+

Enter your Real Debrid API key. This can be found in your Real Debrid account settings.

Torrentio

+

Enable or disable Torrentio integration.
